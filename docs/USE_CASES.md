# Redrise Use Cases

> Practical examples for daily operations using Workspace, Flows, and Tasks.

## 1. Sales Qualification Workspace

- Workspace: Sales Qualification.
- Goal: qualify inbound leads, score opportunities, and route accounts to the right owner.
- Flow: Lead Intake.
- Tasks: capture lead data, enrich company profile, validate contact channel.
- Flow: Deal Scoring.
- Tasks: score budget fit, score urgency, flag missing information.
- Flow: Rep Assignment.
- Tasks: assign owner, notify sales team, schedule first follow-up.

## 2. Customer Onboarding Workspace

- Workspace: Customer Onboarding.
- Goal: turn a signed customer into an active account with clear handoff and training.
- Flow: Account Setup.
- Tasks: create customer record, validate contract scope, configure access.
- Flow: Kickoff Planning.
- Tasks: schedule kickoff, prepare agenda, assign internal owner.
- Flow: Training Follow-up.
- Tasks: send training materials, collect questions, confirm first value milestone.

## 3. Support Triage Workspace

- Workspace: Support Triage.
- Goal: classify support requests and reduce time to first response.
- Flow: Ticket Intake.
- Tasks: read issue, classify priority, detect missing context.
- Flow: Resolution Routing.
- Tasks: route to support, engineering, or customer success; notify assignee; set SLA.
- Flow: Closure Review.
- Tasks: validate answer, request customer confirmation, update knowledge base.

## 4. Finance Operations Workspace

- Workspace: Finance Operations.
- Goal: manage invoice review, approvals, and exception handling.
- Flow: Invoice Intake.
- Tasks: extract invoice data, match vendor, detect duplicates.
- Flow: Approval Path.
- Tasks: route approval, collect comments, mark approved or blocked.
- Flow: Payment Readiness.
- Tasks: verify bank data, export payment batch, notify finance owner.

## 5. HR Hiring Workspace

- Workspace: HR Hiring.
- Goal: screen candidates and keep interview steps organized.
- Flow: Candidate Screening.
- Tasks: parse resume, compare requirements, shortlist candidates.
- Flow: Interview Coordination.
- Tasks: schedule interview, notify interviewers, prepare scorecard.
- Flow: Offer Decision.
- Tasks: consolidate feedback, request approval, send offer or rejection.

## 6. Marketing Campaign Workspace

- Workspace: Marketing Campaign.
- Goal: plan, execute, and monitor a campaign from brief to reporting.
- Flow: Campaign Brief.
- Tasks: define audience, define message, prepare assets checklist.
- Flow: Launch Checklist.
- Tasks: review landing page, approve copy, schedule publication.
- Flow: Performance Review.
- Tasks: collect metrics, identify blockers, create optimization tasks.

## 7. Compliance Review Workspace

- Workspace: Compliance Review.
- Goal: track policy reviews with human approval points.
- Flow: Policy Intake.
- Tasks: register policy, identify owner, attach reference documents.
- Flow: Risk Review.
- Tasks: flag risk areas, assign reviewer, request missing evidence.
- Flow: Approval Trail.
- Tasks: record approval, notify stakeholders, archive decision notes.

## Operational Pattern

- Create a Workspace first to define the operational context.
- Create one or more Flows inside the Workspace to model the process.
- Add cards in Flow Builder to represent the steps of each Flow.
- Create Tasks to execute or review work associated with the Workspace.
- Monitor Dashboard blocks for live status across Workspaces, Flows, Tasks, and Agents.
